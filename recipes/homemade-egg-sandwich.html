Homemade Egg Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices of bread of your choice
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheese
  • Spinach
  • Bacon, sausage or panchetta

Instructions:

  1. Meats and veggies

    Add your cooking oil to a pan and heat it to medium heat. Once the oil has heated up, add your meats first, then your veggies once the meat is almost done.

  2. Fry the eggs and toast the bread

    Note: while your eggs are frying, toast your bread.

    Once your meats and veggies are done, crack an egg directly on top of them. After about a minute, flip the egg. Once both sides are cooked, set the egg aside. Repeat these two steps for however many eggs you want to use.

  3. Put it all together

    Once your bread is toasted and both eggs are done, season the eggs however you want. Then place them on the toast, and place the cheese on top of each egg.

  4. Enjoy!
